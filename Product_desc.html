ABOUT THE DESIGNER

Named after the mothers of design duo Jack McCollough and Lazaro Hernandez, Proenza Schouler began in 2002 as a senior project at Parsons School of Design. Awarded the prestigious Perry Ellis Golden Thimble award, the label soon grew beyond the initial collection to include a range of cutting-edge ready-to-wear apparel, shoes, and handbags. Defined by a fusion of craftsmanship with a contemporary feel, Proenza Schouler clothing takes inspiration from art and youth culture to make refined and bold pieces that will revitalize your collection.
